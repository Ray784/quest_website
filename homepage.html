About Cse Quest

Quest is an annual technical fest, proudly presented by the Computer Science department of JNTUH. With this two day long event, we bring to you a conglomerate of unique idea, innovative skill and unlimited fun. A National level technical colloquium, Quest includes technical contests like technical events, workshops and cultural events. From challenging the coder in you at coding events to bring out the scholar in you at paper presentations, the events are designed to tech-train young minds. Be a part of the Quest and experience the ardor, for this is an event that truly justifies it’s name and this is the time , it’s going to be the best ever. .
